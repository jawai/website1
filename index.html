Biography

Battle Tested

Before founding Palpatine LLP in 2016, Sheev worked for a major law firm in Toronto where he represented clients in court on their family and employment matters.

Client Focused

Unhappy with how most law firms manage and treat their clients, Sheev decided to start his own firm where he could focus on giving clients the best service possible by leveraging his previous career as a general manager at a prestigious global hotel chain.

After Hours
